Complete Xbox
UI Package

Xbox 360 and Xbox One UI sprite sheets.

This package is great for developers looking to publish on the Xbox platform and wish to quickly intergrate UI functionality. Within this pack is every Xbox One and Xbox 360 button found on the controller, and in addition, several different styles of A,B,X,Y buttons and colors. Also, an Xbox One and Xbox 360 controller sprite is included, with the added bonus of a predesign controller layout sprite for both console generations.
