Project
Restaurant

This is the current project I'm developing under the name Project Restaurant. The product name will not be disclosed until further developement and the studio can get rights to the name.

As of right now, I am in the pre-demo and pre-alpha stages, however, expect to have a demo ready within the next six months.

Game Concept

This game is a comedic take on the events of WW2 involving types of beans battling it out for their nations. Players will create and customize their characters all the way down to choosing a "race" or type of bean. The type of beans to choose from will be lima beans, pinto beans, black beans, and a red kidney beans.
