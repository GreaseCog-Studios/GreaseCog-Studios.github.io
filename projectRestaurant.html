Project
Restaurant

This is the current project I'm developing under the name Project Restaurant. The product name will not be disclosed until further developement and the studio can get rights to the name.

As of right now, I am in the pre-demo and pre-alpha stages, however, expect to have a demo ready within the next six months.

Game Concept

Players choose from several types of beans and can customize them with various cosmetic items. After selecting thier player and the peferred map, the players are throw into total chaos as they battle their way through bean-on-bean violence from their friend's couch. There are multiple weapons to master, from a simple laser rifle to a giant laser minigun, and players can get even more devious with explosives meant to blast any side dish to the next era. This game will run on the XboxOne/PS4 and is up to four players. I hope to be able to launch to Xbox Series X and PS5 as well.
